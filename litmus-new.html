Become a Better Email Marketer


Learn the latest tips, tricks, and best practices for email design, strategy, and development at Litmus Live in Boston, London, or San Francisco.

Find out more

Session highlights include:


BOSTON

How to Plan SMAARRRT Lifecycle Emails

Janet Choi, Customer.io

Discover this simple strategy to map out customer-centric lifecycle emails and show how you can best set them up for success with actionable goals.

 

BOSTON

How it Works: Mobile Email Device Detection

Steven Collins, Rebel

Learn how to display device-specific content with this device detection technique, and understand the theory behind how it works.


LONDON

Email as Art: Taking Email Design to the Next Level

Sam Beddoes, Action Rocket

Learn how to move email design beyond ESP templates and how to use standard practices to create something that feels beautiful and unique.

 

LONDON

5 Things I Learned About Relevancy From Sending 53 Million Emails

Becs Rivett-Kemm, Conversio

Learn how including video tutorials, preference centers, and plain text messages can improve retention and reduce unsubscribes.


SAN FRANCISCO

Not Just Pretty Numbers: Making Data-Driven Design Decisions

Alex Kelly, MailChimp

Explore how visual elements like layout, color, and text length can impact campaign engagement, and learn some best practices for email design.

 

SAN FRANCISCO

Localization at Scale

Erin Turner, Udemy

Learn how to tackle the technical challenges that come with localization, with a specific focus on scalability and automation.
